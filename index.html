Why English

English is the largest global language used for communication across the world. Knowing English is a pre-requisite for gainful employment anywhere in the world. English skills are required for you even for migration to the west. Most inventions and products are made and marketed in English. India has recognised its importance and has made English an official language in addition to Hindi. English is the common thread in multinational companies which operate across the Globe. English allows you into the elite circles of society. Ideas can be sold and marketed only in Classic.
