Why English!!

The importance of English in today's world can not be overstated:
‑English is the language used in international communications and business.
‑English opens up better academic and career opportunities.
‑Knowing English is a pre-requisite for gainful employment anywhere in the world including India.
‑India has recognised its importance and has made English an official language in addition to Hindi.
‑English can be considered most useful language in the world today:   ‑English is the language of science, aviation, medicine, tourism etc.   ‑English is the language of Internet and computers.   ‑Most inventions and products are made and marketed in English.   ‑Multinational companies operate and transact in English.   ‑Travelling or migration anywhere in the world or withing India becomes easier with the knowledge of English.
